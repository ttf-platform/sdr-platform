# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B founders and early-stage sales leads (teams of 1–10) running outbound without a
dedicated SDR. They arrive already skeptical — burned by Artisan/11x promises or
underwhelmed by tool-heavy stacks. Job to be done: find a cold outreach system that
runs reliably without daily babysitting. They need to believe Sentra is different
before they sign up for the free trial.

## Product Purpose

Sentra automates the full SDR workflow — from cold list to booked meetings — without
a human SDR. Landing page success means a skeptical founder reads the page and thinks
"this is the first AI SDR tool that sounds like it was built by someone who
understands the job." Conversion is a free trial signup started — Sentra has no demo
gate.

## Brand Personality

Confident, founder-built, unhyped. Voice is direct and specific — no hyperbole, no
buzzwords, no AI-utopia promises. Tone is founder-to-founder: we've done this, we
know what doesn't work, here's what does. Emotional goal: credibility through
restraint. Visitors should leave feeling informed, not sold to.

Mix register: polished in execution (typography, layout, motion precision), scrappy in
voice (founder-built phrasings, no marketing-speak).

## Anti-references

- B2B Rocket / Outreach / Salesloft: enterprise corporate gloss, "Skyrocket sales
  with AI Agents", glowing orbs, demo-gated everything
- Artisan / 11x: AI-utopia framing ("Hire Ava, your AI BDR"), purple gradients,
  anthropomorphized AI agents as a pitch
- Apollo: generic dashboard-screenshot SaaS, feature-list-as-hero
- Lemlist: over-polished startup template, warm-orange personality without substance
- Stripe-lookalike: cream + serif italic minimalism — becoming a B2B cliché
- Linear-mimicry: dark mode tech-precise but cold and impersonal

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over promise** — Every claim is specific and verifiable. "Books 3–8
   meetings per week" beats "Skyrocket your pipeline."
2. **Show the product** — Skeptical founders trust interfaces over copy. The product
   earns credibility; words just open the door.
3. **Restraint as confidence** — What Sentra doesn't say matters as much as what it
   does. No buzzwords, no "revolutionary", no "AI-powered" as a selling point.
4. **Founder voice** — Every line reads like the person who built the product talking
   to the person who would use it.
5. **Anti-template** — Every layout choice is explainable. If it can be described as
   "like most SaaS landing pages," it's wrong.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Animations are enhancement-only, never load-bearing. `useReducedMotion`
already in place — extend to all future animations. Avoid color-only encoding (pair
color with text and/or shape). Consistent h1/h2/h3 hierarchy, alt text on all visuals,
aria-labels on CTAs. Color contrast at AA for all body copy and interactive elements.
