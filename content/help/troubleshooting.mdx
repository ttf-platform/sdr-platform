# Troubleshooting common issues

Find your issue below. If it's not listed, open the AI assistant in your dashboard (bottom-right help button).

## Mailbox disconnected. How do I reconnect?

Go to **Settings, Mailboxes**. Click the disconnected mailbox, then **Reconnect**. Re-complete the OAuth flow for Gmail or Outlook.

Mailboxes disconnect when you change your account password, revoke app access, or when a token expires. Reconnection takes under a minute.

- Deep dive: [Linking your mailbox](/help/linking-your-mailbox)

## Drafts aren't appearing in the Approval Queue

Check these in order:

1. **Campaign is active?** Go to Campaigns and confirm the campaign status is "Active," not "Paused" or "Draft."
2. **Prospects are added?** Open the campaign and check the Prospects tab. If it's empty, add prospects and re-launch.
3. **Signal attached?** If the campaign has a Signal, drafts only generate when the signal fires. Check the Signal's match history.
4. **Queue is filtered?** The Approval Queue may be filtered by campaign or status. Clear all filters.

If none of these apply, wait an hour. Mirvo processes prospect batches asynchronously.

## Prospect CSV import failed

The most common cause: the required columns are missing or misnamed.

Required: `email`, `first_name`, `company` (case-insensitive). Optional extras work as merge fields.

Other causes:

- Encoding issues: save the CSV as UTF-8 before uploading
- Special characters in headers (remove spaces, parentheses, etc.)
- Email addresses with formatting issues (extra spaces, angle brackets)

Download the import template from the import dialog for a guaranteed-working format.

- Deep dive: [Picking your prospects](/help/picking-your-prospects)

## OAuth fails for Gmail or Outlook

**Gmail:** Check that your Google Workspace admin hasn't blocked third-party app access. Ask your admin to allowlist Mirvo's app ID, or use a personal Google account for testing.

**Outlook:** If your organization uses conditional access policies or MFA requirements, the OAuth flow may fail silently. Use a mailbox that isn't behind a strict conditional access policy, or have your IT team add an exception.

For both: make sure you're completing the consent screen fully (don't close the popup before it redirects back).

- Deep dive: [Linking your mailbox](/help/linking-your-mailbox)

## Signal not firing as expected

Check the signal conditions: go to **Signals, [your signal], Edit conditions**. Common issues:

- Keywords too specific (e.g., exact phrase not appearing in web content)
- Signal source not enabled for your plan
- Scan hasn't run since the event occurred (signals scan nightly)

Test the signal using the **Preview matches** button before attaching it to a campaign.

- Deep dive: [Creating custom signals](/help/creating-custom-signals)

## Email landed in spam. What to check.

In order of likelihood:

1. **Prospect's own filters:** Some corporate email servers are aggressive. This isn't always fixable.
2. **Subject line:** Contains spam trigger words (see [Subject line best practices](/help/subject-line-best-practices))
3. **Link density:** More than 2 links in a short email increases spam risk
4. **Custom domain not verified:** If you're using a custom sending domain and SPF/DKIM/DMARC aren't verified, spam placement increases sharply
5. **Content:** Attachments, images, or HTML-heavy formatting all increase spam risk. Plain text or minimal HTML performs better.

- Deep dive: [Why emails go to spam](/help/why-emails-go-to-spam)

## Reply not categorized correctly

Open the reply in your inbox, click the category label, and select the correct one. Manual overrides are applied immediately and improve future categorization.

## Can't add custom domain. DNS issues.

The most common causes:

- You added the record under the wrong type (TXT vs CNAME)
- You added it to the root domain when Mirvo expects a subdomain record
- Your DNS provider has caching that delays propagation

Try deleting and re-adding the record exactly as shown in the wizard. Wait at least 30 minutes before checking again.

- Deep dive: [Adding a sending domain](/help/adding-a-sending-domain)
