# 7-step setup walkthrough

This is the master onboarding doc. Each step has its own deep-dive article where useful. The walkthrough below is the recommended order.

Total active time: around 20 minutes. First drafts can appear in your Approval Queue within an hour of completing step 6.

<Callout type="info">
Already signed in? Open your [dashboard](/dashboard) and follow along. The onboarding checklist in the top-right tracks your progress.
</Callout>

## Step 1. Link your sending mailbox

Click **Settings, Mailboxes, Add mailbox**. Choose Gmail or Outlook and complete the OAuth flow. This takes about 30 seconds.

Your real inbox stays separate from Mirvo's outbound traffic. Replies land in Mirvo's inbox, not your personal inbox.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Settings, Mailboxes page with "Add mailbox" button highlighted, showing Gmail and Outlook options' caption='Settings, Mailboxes page with "Add mailbox" button highlighted, showing Gmail and Outlook options' />

Deep dive: [Linking your mailbox](/help/linking-your-mailbox)

## Step 2. Set up your Master ICP

Click **Settings, Master ICP**. Fill in:

- **Who you sell to** (job titles, company size, industry)
- **What pain you solve** (specific outcome, not feature list)
- **Tone of voice** (3-5 examples of how you'd talk to a prospect)

Master ICP is the ground truth Mirvo uses for every email it drafts. Time spent here pays back in every downstream draft.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Master ICP form with example values filled in' caption='Master ICP form with example values filled in' />

Deep dive: [Your Master ICP](/help/master-icp)

## Step 3. (Optional) Connect a custom sending domain

By default, Mirvo sends from a shared sending domain with managed reputation. This works on day one and requires no setup.

For better long-term deliverability and brand consistency, connect a custom domain you own. Click **Settings, Sending Domains, Add domain** and follow the 3-step wizard (DNS records auto-generated, copy them to your DNS provider).

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Sending Domains 3-step wizard, showing DNS records to add' caption='Sending Domains 3-step wizard, showing DNS records to add' />

Deep dive: [Adding a sending domain](/help/adding-a-sending-domain)

## Step 4. Pick your prospects

Two ways to populate your prospect list:

- **Import contacts.** Upload a CSV, or paste rows. Required columns: email, first name, company. Other columns become merge fields.
- **Search Mirvo's prospect database.** Filter by industry, role, company size, geography, tech stack. Build a list inside Mirvo, no CSV required.

You can use both in the same campaign.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Prospects page with toggle between "Import" and "Search" tabs' caption='Prospects page with toggle between "Import" and "Search" tabs' />

Deep dive: [Picking your prospects](/help/picking-your-prospects)

## Step 5. Create your first campaign

Click **Campaigns, New campaign**. Configure:

- **Target prospects** (from step 4)
- **Email sequence** (first email + follow-ups, AI drafts each)
- **Optional: add a Signal** for buying-moment targeting. Without a signal, the campaign drafts for every targeted prospect.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Campaign creation form with prospect selection, sequence steps, and optional Signal section' caption='Campaign creation form with prospect selection, sequence steps, and optional Signal section' />

Deep dive: [Create your first campaign](/help/create-your-first-campaign)

## Step 6. Review and approve drafts

Drafts appear in the **Approval Queue** within an hour of launch (often within minutes). For each draft:

- **Read it.** Does the personalization make sense? Does the ask fit the prospect?
- **Edit if needed.** Inline edits are saved instantly.
- **Approve.** Mirvo sends through managed deliverability.

You stay in control. Nothing goes out without your approval.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Approval Queue with a draft email expanded, showing AI-generated personalization and edit/approve buttons' caption='Approval Queue with a draft email expanded, showing AI-generated personalization and edit/approve buttons' />

Deep dive: [Approval Queue workflow](/help/approval-queue-workflow)

## Step 7. Monitor replies and iterate

Replies land in your **Mirvo inbox**, sorted by intent:

- **Positive** (interested, asking questions)
- **Objection** (push back, ask for proof)
- **Out of office** (try again later, auto-detected)
- **Unsubscribe** (auto-suppressed for future campaigns)

Use the **Campaign Analytics** view to track open rate, reply rate, and positive reply rate per Signal. Iterate on your Master ICP and Signals based on what's working.
