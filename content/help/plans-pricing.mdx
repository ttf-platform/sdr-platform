# Plans and pricing

Mirvo has three plans. This page explains what's included in each and helps you pick the right one.

<!-- PRICING_DATA -->

## Plans overview

| | Starter | Pro | Power |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | $149/mo | $299/mo | $399/mo |
| Lifetime prospects | 2,500 | 10,000 | Unlimited |
| Emails per month | 1,000 | 5,000 | 20,000 |
| Prospect Credits/mo | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 |

*Subject to change pre-launch. Current pricing always shown in Settings, Billing.*

## What's in every plan

All plans include:

- Unlimited campaigns and Signals
- AI-drafted personalized emails (not templates)
- Approval Queue with inline editing
- Mirvo inbox with intent sorting
- Managed deliverability infrastructure (no warmup wait)
- Gmail and Outlook OAuth integration
- Reply analytics

## What each plan is for

**Starter ($149/mo):** Solo founder or small team just starting outbound. 1,000 emails per month covers about 3-5 active campaigns at steady state. Good if you're still learning your ICP and want to validate before scaling.

**Pro ($299/mo):** The most popular plan. 5,000 emails per month and 10,000 lifetime prospects supports a serious outbound motion. Right for founders who've found product-market fit and are scaling pipeline.

**Power ($399/mo):** High-volume teams. Unlimited lifetime prospects and 20,000 emails per month. Right for companies with multiple ICPs or SDR teams using Mirvo.

## Understanding the three caps

**Lifetime prospects:** Total unique prospects ever added to your workspace (imported or searched). Once a prospect is in your workspace, they count forever, even if removed from campaigns.

**Emails per month:** Total approved emails sent in a calendar month. Resets on the 1st. Includes follow-ups.

**Prospect Credits per month:** Credits used when searching Mirvo's prospect database. Each search result that you add to a campaign costs one credit. Resets on the 1st.

<Callout type="info">
Imported prospects (CSV or paste) don't consume Prospect Credits. Credits only apply to Mirvo's built-in prospect search.
</Callout>

## Upgrading

Go to **Settings, Billing, Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately. You're charged the prorated difference for the current billing period.
