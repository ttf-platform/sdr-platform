# Quotas explained

Mirvo has three distinct caps. They track different things and reset on different schedules. This article explains each one.

{/* LIMITS_DATA */}

## The three caps

### Lifetime prospects

**What it counts:** Every unique prospect ever added to your workspace, whether imported via CSV, pasted, or added from search.

**When it resets:** Never. Lifetime means lifetime. Once a prospect is counted, they count forever, even if you delete them from all campaigns or remove them from your workspace entirely.

**Why it exists:** This cap reflects the cost of prospect data enrichment and storage. The number of prospects you've ever processed is the unit.

**Watch for:** If you're importing large lists frequently, this fills faster than expected. Don't import prospects you don't plan to use. Quality over quantity.

### Emails per month

**What it counts:** Every approved email that leaves Mirvo, including first emails and all follow-up steps.

**When it resets:** The 1st of each calendar month, regardless of when your billing cycle started.

**Why it exists:** Email sending infrastructure has direct per-message costs. This cap aligns usage with plan tier.

**Watch for:** If you're running multiple high-volume campaigns simultaneously, this can deplete faster than expected. Check Settings, Billing to see month-to-date usage.

<Callout type="info">
Emails in the Approval Queue don't count toward the monthly cap until they're approved and sent. Drafts sitting in the queue don't cost anything.
</Callout>

### Prospect Credits per month

**What it counts:** Credits used when you search Mirvo's prospect database and add results to a campaign. Each result you add costs one credit.

**When it resets:** The 1st of each calendar month.

**Why it exists:** Prospect database lookups have a per-record cost from the underlying data provider.

**Important:** Prospect Credits only apply to Mirvo's built-in search. Importing your own list via CSV or paste does not consume credits.

## Checking your current usage

Go to **Settings, Billing**. The usage panel shows:

- Lifetime prospects: used / plan limit
- Emails this month: used / monthly limit, resets in X days
- Prospect Credits this month: used / monthly limit

## What happens when you hit a cap

**Lifetime prospects cap reached:** You can no longer add new prospects (import or search). Existing campaigns with already-imported prospects continue to run. To add more, upgrade your plan.

**Monthly email cap reached:** New approvals are queued but held. Emails resume sending on the 1st when the cap resets. Alternatively, upgrade your plan for a higher monthly limit.

**Prospect Credits exhausted:** Prospect database search is disabled for the month. Importing via CSV still works. Credits reset on the 1st.

## Upgrading your plan

Go to **Settings, Billing, Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately. The higher limits apply from the moment of upgrade, not from the next billing cycle.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does removing a prospect from a campaign free up my lifetime quota?**

No. Lifetime prospects count all unique prospects ever added, regardless of current campaign status.

**If I import the same email address twice, does it count twice?**

No. Duplicate email addresses in imports are deduplicated. The lifetime prospect count tracks unique email addresses.

**Do follow-up emails cost the same as first emails?**

Yes. Each sent email, whether step 1 or step 3 in a sequence, counts as one email toward the monthly cap.
