# Create your first campaign

This article walks through creating and launching a campaign end to end. Prerequisite: your [ICP is filled in](/dashboard/profile#icp) and at least one mailbox is connected.

## Step 1. Open the campaign wizard

Click **Campaigns, New campaign** in the sidebar. The wizard opens.

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/create-your-first-campaign-1.png' alt='Campaigns list page with "New campaign" button highlighted' caption='Campaigns list page with "New campaign" button highlighted' />

## Step 2. Name your campaign

Pick a name that describes the target and angle. You'll reference this when reading Approval Queue drafts.

Good examples: "Series A SaaS founders Q3" or "CHRO at healthcare 500-2000 emp."

## Step 3. Select your prospects

Two options:

**Import:** Upload a CSV or paste rows. Required columns: email, first_name, company. Additional columns (role, industry, etc.) become available to the AI as personalization context.

**Search:** Use Mirvo's prospect database. Filter by role, industry, company size, geography, and tech stack. Add results to the campaign directly.

You can combine both: import your own list and add searched prospects to the same campaign.

<Callout type="tip">
For your first campaign, start with 20-50 prospects. Smaller batches let you calibrate the ICP and review drafts before scaling.
</Callout>

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/create-your-first-campaign-2.png' alt='Prospect selection step showing Import and Search tabs with filters open' caption='Prospect selection step showing Import and Search tabs with filters open' />

## Step 4. Configure your email sequence

Add the first email, then optional follow-up steps.

- **First email:** Mirvo drafts this using your ICP and prospect data. You approve before it sends.
- **Follow-ups:** Set the delay (e.g., 3 days, 7 days) and tone guidance per step. Mirvo drafts each follow-up in context of the previous email.
- **Sequence length:** 1-5 steps. Most effective campaigns use 2-3 steps.

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/create-your-first-campaign-3.png' alt='Sequence builder showing first email and two follow-up steps with delay settings' caption='Sequence builder showing first email and two follow-up steps with delay settings' />

## Step 5. (Optional) Add a Signal

Under "Buying signal targeting," click **Add Signal**. Choose an existing Signal or create a new one.

Without a Signal, Mirvo drafts for every prospect in the campaign immediately.

With a Signal, drafts are generated only when a prospect in scope matches the Signal condition. Other prospects in scope get no draft until the signal fires, or if you also run the campaign without signal targeting.

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/create-your-first-campaign-4.png' alt='Signal selection dropdown in campaign wizard with "Skip for now" option visible' caption='Signal selection dropdown in campaign wizard with "Skip for now" option visible' />

## Step 6. Review and launch

The summary screen shows your prospect count, sequence steps, and Signal configuration. Click **Launch campaign**.

Mirvo begins processing prospects. For campaigns without signals, drafts appear in the Approval Queue within minutes to an hour depending on queue depth.

<Screenshot placeholder alt='Campaign summary confirmation screen before launch' caption='Campaign summary confirmation screen before launch' />

## After launch

Check your **Approval Queue** to find the first drafts. Read them, edit if needed, and approve.

Nothing sends until you approve. You stay in control of every email.
