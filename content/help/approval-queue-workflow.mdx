# Approval Queue workflow

The Approval Queue is where you review AI-drafted emails before they send. Nothing leaves Mirvo without your explicit approval. This article covers the daily workflow.

## Where to find it

**Approval Queue** in the sidebar, or via the badge on the bell icon when new drafts are ready.

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/approval-queue-workflow-1.png' alt='Approval Queue list view showing multiple drafts with campaign labels and timestamps' caption='Approval Queue list view showing multiple drafts with campaign labels and timestamps' />

## Reading a draft

Each draft shows:

- **Prospect:** Name, company, role
- **Subject line** (AI-generated, editable)
- **Email body** (AI-generated, editable)
- **Signal context** (if the draft was triggered by a Signal, the match details appear here)
- **Campaign label**

Expand a draft to read the full email. The AI personalizes based on your ICP and the prospect's context. Look for:

- Does the pain angle match what this prospect actually cares about?
- Is the company/role reference accurate?
- Is the tone right?

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/approval-queue-workflow-2.png' alt='Draft expanded view with subject, body, and signal context panel open' caption='Draft expanded view with subject, body, and signal context panel open' />

## Editing a draft

Click anywhere in the subject line or body to edit inline. Changes are saved automatically.

Common edits:

- Tighten a long opening sentence
- Swap a generic pain for a more specific one you know about this prospect
- Adjust the CTA if the timing or ask feels off

<Callout type="tip">
The AI learns from your edits over time. Consistent changes in a specific direction (e.g., always removing a certain phrase) will be reflected in future drafts for that campaign.
</Callout>

## Approving

Click **Approve**. The email enters the send queue and goes out through Mirvo's deliverability infrastructure within the next sending window.

## Rejecting or regenerating

- **Reject:** Removes the draft from the queue. The prospect won't receive this email. Useful if the personalization is irrelevant or the prospect isn't a fit.
- **Regenerate:** Discards the current draft and requests a new one. Mirvo re-drafts using the same context. Useful when the approach was off but the prospect is still a good target.

## Bulk actions

Select multiple drafts using the checkboxes on the left. Bulk approve, bulk reject, or bulk assign to a different campaign.

<Screenshot src='/help/screenshots/approval-queue-workflow-3.png' alt='Bulk selection with checkboxes checked and bulk action bar at bottom showing Approve All, Reject All options' caption='Bulk selection with checkboxes checked and bulk action bar at bottom showing Approve All, Reject All options' />

## Filtering

Filter the queue by:

- Campaign
- Signal (show only signal-triggered drafts)
- Status (pending, approved, rejected, sent)
- Date range

This helps when managing multiple campaigns simultaneously.

## After approval

Once sent, the draft moves to the campaign's **Sent** view. When the prospect replies, the reply lands in your Mirvo inbox, categorized by intent.
