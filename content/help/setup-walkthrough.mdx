# Setup walkthrough

This is the master onboarding doc. Each step has its own deep-dive article where useful. Follow the order below.

Total active time: around 20 minutes. First drafts can appear in your Approval Queue within an hour of finishing the walkthrough.

<Callout type="info">
Already signed in? Open your [dashboard](/dashboard) and follow along. A setup checklist pinned in the bottom-left corner of every dashboard page tracks your progress.
</Callout>

## Define your ICP

Open the **Profile & ICP** tab. Two sections live there: **Offer** (`#offre`) and **Ideal Customer Profile** (`#icp`). The fastest way to fill both is the auto-fill button: paste your website URL in the Offer section and Mirvo pre-fills your product description, value proposition, industry, company size, and every field in the ICP block in a single click. Review, tweak, save.

Master ICP is the ground truth Mirvo uses for every email it drafts. Time spent here pays back on every downstream draft, so the two-minute review after auto-fill is worth it.

Direct link: [dashboard/profile#icp](/dashboard/profile#icp)

## Connect your sending inbox

Open **Settings, Sending Domains** and connect Gmail or Outlook through the OAuth flow. Takes about 30 seconds. Replies land in Mirvo's inbox, not your personal one, so your real inbox stays quiet.

If you plan to scale volume, add a dedicated sending domain later on the same page. Mirvo warms it up in the background while your connected mailbox keeps sending, so there's no downtime and no separate step to complete before day one.

## Create your first campaign

Click **Campaigns, New campaign**. Mirvo AI drafts the full sequence for you: opening email, follow-ups, cadence. The AI reads your Master ICP, so the sharper that is, the better the drafts.

Optional: attach a Signal at creation time to narrow drafting to prospects hitting a buying moment. Without a Signal, the campaign drafts for every targeted prospect.

## Add your prospects

Two ways to populate the list:

- **Import.** Upload a CSV or paste rows. Required columns: email, first name, company. Other columns become merge fields the AI can pull into drafts.
- **AI discovery.** Filter Mirvo's prospect database by industry, role, company size, geography, and tech stack. No CSV required.

You can mix both inside the same campaign.

Direct link: [dashboard/prospects](/dashboard/prospects)

## Review, approve & send

Drafts appear in the **Approval Queue** shortly after you launch the campaign. For each one:

- **Read.** Does the personalization make sense? Does the ask fit the prospect?
- **Edit inline.** Any change is saved instantly.
- **Approve.** Approved drafts go into the send queue and leave from your connected mailbox on their scheduled cadence.

Nothing goes out without your approval. Once the rhythm feels right, you can switch specific campaigns to auto-approve and step back to the analytics view.

To open it, go to your campaign detail page and click the Approval Queue tab.
